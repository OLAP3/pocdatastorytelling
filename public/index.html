This is a demo of a simple data story creation. Your story begins by creating a goal. Then you can create acts and episodes by writing text and enriching them with answers to analytical questions. In this demo, questions are answered by SQL queries over the SSB schema. Validating the answers results in an observation for which you need to define your story protagonists and messages. Finally, render your story and download it as a PDF file.
